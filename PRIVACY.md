# Bright Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2, 2026

Bright is a daily reading app for solutions journalism, built by Appian Studios. This policy explains what information Bright collects, what it doesn't, and how it works.

We've kept it short on purpose: Bright is designed to be a quiet, considered alternative to the rest of the news app landscape, and that extends to how we treat your data — by not collecting any.

## What Bright doesn't do

- We do not have user accounts, sign-in, or registration.
- We do not collect personal information.
- We do not track you across apps or websites.
- We do not show advertisements.
- We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing SDKs.
- We do not sell or share data with anyone.
- We do not use device identifiers (IDFA, advertising IDs, etc.).
- We do not collect your location.

There is nothing for us to sell or share, because we don't collect anything.

## What Bright stores on your device

Bright saves a small set of preferences locally on your device using your operating system's standard storage (AsyncStorage on iOS and Android). These never leave your device and are not transmitted to us:

- Your selected start screen (Today or Globe)
- Whether you've enabled the daily notification, and at what time
- Your selected notification tone
- Whether you've completed the onboarding flow
- A small cache of the most recent story list, for instant warm-boot on next launch

You can clear all of this by deleting the app from your device.

## What Bright sends over the network

When you use Bright, the app makes a small number of network requests to display content. We do not include any user identifier, account, IP-address-based fingerprint, or location in these requests beyond what is automatically present in any standard HTTP connection.

- **Bright's content server** (`raw.githubusercontent.com`): downloads the day's curated story list. The server is hosted on GitHub. Bright sends only the minimum required to fetch the file.
- **Country border data** (`raw.githubusercontent.com`): downloads a public dataset of world country outlines used by the 3D globe.
- **Original article links**: when you tap "Read at [source]" or share a story, you are directed to the publisher's website (Mongabay, Reasons to be Cheerful, The Guardian, etc.). Bright does not see what you do once you arrive there. Each publisher has its own privacy policy.
- **Weather data** (`api.open-meteo.com`): when you tap a country marker on the globe, Bright requests current weather for that country's coordinates. Only the country's coordinates are sent — never your device's location.

## Notifications

If you opt in to the daily notification, your device's operating system schedules a local notification at your chosen time. The notification text is generated entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to us when it fires, and we cannot push messages to your device from a server.

## Children's privacy

Bright is age-rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app does not contain user-generated content, social features, advertising, or in-app purchases.

## Third-party services

Bright does not embed third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, or marketing SDKs of any kind. The only third-party servers Bright contacts are listed in "What Bright sends over the network" above, and contact with them is limited to fetching public content.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top, and the change will be visible at the URL hosting this document. The app's About screen links here, so you can read the current version at any time.

## Your rights

Because Bright does not collect, store, or process personal information about you on our servers, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, delete, or transfer in response to a data-subject request. If you believe this is mistaken, or if you have any concern about your privacy in connection with Bright, please contact us at the address below and we will respond within a reasonable time.
